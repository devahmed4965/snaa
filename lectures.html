Our Lectures and Courses

Explore a variety of topics in Islamic religious sciences specifically designed for beginners.

Creed

Introduction to Islamic Creed (Aqidah)

A comprehensive overview of the pillars of faith (belief in Allah, His angels, His books, His messengers, the Last Day, and divine decree). A simplified explanation of the most important basic concepts in Islamic creed and their significance.

Who is this course for?

For beginners and those wishing to understand the foundations of Islam correctly.

Jurisprudence

Fundamentals of Fiqh (Worship)

A practical and simplified explanation of the rulings on purification (Wudu, Ghusl, Tayammum), prayer (its pillars, obligations, sunnahs), Zakat (its conditions, recipients), and fasting (its rulings and invalidators).

Who is this course for?

For every Muslim who wants to learn how to perform basic acts of worship correctly.

Biography

Lessons from the Prophet's Biography (Seerah)

A review of the most prominent milestones in the life of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ from his birth until his death, focusing on the educational, ethical, and moral lessons learned from his stances and life events.

Who is this course for?

For those wishing to learn about the Prophet of Islam ﷺ and emulate his character and methodology.

Exegesis

Introduction to Quranic Sciences and Tafsir

An introduction to the sciences of the Holy Quran, how it was revealed and compiled, and the most important methodologies of commentators in understanding and reflecting on its verses. Practical application on the interpretation of some short Surahs.

Who is this course for?

For those interested in understanding the Holy Quran and the principles of its interpretation.
